HOW THE JOURNEY BEGAN.......

My name is Shivani Suralia, founder of Winnie's Adventures alongside co-founder Parag Suralia. My journey in childcare began while I was still at school, when I completed my work experience placement in a nursery. From that moment, I knew early years education was my passion. I continued my studies to build my knowledge, skills, and understanding of child development, and since then I have never looked back. Supporting children, helping them grow, and making a positive difference in their lives has always been at the heart of everything I do.

I completed my BA honours in Early Childhood Studies in 2009, and hold a diploma in Counselling children and Adolescents. I stepped into the childcare industry in 2009 and since worked across a wide range of settings, including large chain nurseries, independent nurseries, social enterprises, and charity organisations. Over the past 15 years, I have gained extensive experience within reputable early years settings, with more than 10 years spent in management roles leading teams and supporting high-quality childcare provision.

In 2026, I made the bold decision to leave nursery management and begin my journey as a childminder. Over the years, I began to feel that the true care element within larger nursery settings was slowly being lost due to increasing numbers, pressures, and demands. Children were at risk of becoming just another number, and the simple joy, creativity, and magic of childhood learning was beginning to fade.

Childminding brought me back to what childcare should truly be about — love, warmth, nurturing relationships, and individual care. I love that childminding allows children to grow and learn within a warm, comforting home environment where they feel safe, valued, and genuinely cared for. It also enables me to provide flexible, supportive childcare for working families, building strong partnerships with parents while ensuring every child receives the attention, encouragement, and emotional security they deserve.

At Winnie's Adventures, I believe the early years are the most important foundation of a child's life. Above all else, children need love, care, fun, and a sense of belonging — and that is exactly the environment I aim to create every single day.

LEARNING THROUGH STORIES.

A story opens the mind and takes children into worlds beyond imagination. At Winnie's Adventures, we bring stories to life by creating innovative and exciting adventures inspired by each individual book. Through fun, hands-on, and meaningful experiences, we make learning memorable, engaging, and full of wonder.

Planning at Winnie's Adventures is a balance between adult-led and child-led activities. We believe children are our teachers — they guide us towards their interests and what they are curious to learn about. For example, if children develop an interest in dinosaurs, we use this as an opportunity to extend their learning through creative experiences, play, and exploration.

Our role as educators is to act as facilitators, scaffolding children's learning and supporting them in areas where they may need encouragement or further development. Learning is never rushed or treated as a checklist. We do not believe a child has mastered a skill simply because they have completed it once. Instead, children are given the time, space, and opportunities to explore, practise, and build confidence until they truly master and enjoy the learning experience.

Planning will be shared with parents so they can understand what is being planned for their child and how learning can be continued or extended at home. By working collaboratively together, parents and educators can support children's development more effectively, helping them grow in confidence and achieve their full potential at their own pace.

EATING RIGHT, STAYING BRIGHT

Eating well is key to living a healthy, balanced life, so why not begin building those habits from an early age? Positive eating habits developed during childhood can have long-lasting benefits into adulthood, helping to support better health, more energy, reduced health risks, and overall well-being.

At Winnie's Adventures, we provide warm, nutritious, fresh meals prepared with care each day for our primary school children. Being located in the heart of London, we celebrate diversity by offering a well-balanced menu that includes both high-quality meat and hearty vegetarian options. Our meals are designed to be rich in essential vitamins, minerals, and protein that help support children's growth, focus, immune systems, and energy levels throughout their busy school days.

Introducing children to a variety of healthy, culturally diverse foods from an early age encourages positive eating habits and helps them build a healthy relationship with food for the future.

We believe school mealtimes should be comforting, enjoyable, and nourishing, which is why we do not serve reheated food, leftovers from the previous day, or cold meals.

Healthy habits today, brighter futures tomorrow 💛

WHERE YOUR INVESTMENT BECOMES YOUR CHILD'S FUTURE

FEES

At Winnie's Adventures, our fees are designed to provide high-quality, flexible childcare within a warm and nurturing home environment. Fees include care, learning activities, outings, and freshly prepared nutritious vegetarian meals throughout the day. We aim to offer transparent and fair pricing while supporting families with flexible childcare options and accepted childcare funding where eligible.

Please contact us for our full fee structure and availability.

FUNDING

We understand that childcare costs can be a big commitment for families, which is why there are different childcare funding and support options available to help parents. Depending on your eligibility, you may be able to access funded childcare hours, Tax-Free Childcare, Universal Credit childcare support, or other government schemes designed to make quality childcare more affordable. Eligible families in England may be able to receive funded childcare from 9 months old, 15 hours for all 3 and 4-year-olds, or up to 30 hours for eligible working families.

To find out which childcare funding and support you may be entitled to, we recommend using the government's Childcare Choices eligibility checker:

https://childcare.beststartinlife.gov.uk/

The checker will help you explore the different funding options available and determine what support your family may be eligible for.

Please feel free to contact us if you would like further guidance on the funding options available and how they can be used at Winnie's Adventures.
